Hyderabadi biryani | How to make authentic hyderabadi biryani?

Hyderabadi birayni recipe - Making a good hyderabadi biryani is very simple and this recipe is perfect for a home preparation. This is the easiest biryani since it needs no sauteing of spies or meat. Just marinate the meat with the spices & other ingredients. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over it. Cook on a slow dun process, trapping the steam to retain the aroma.


To get an authentic flavor and taste of biryani, do use all the mentioned ingredients and if desired you can substitue ghee for oil.

This is one of the best hyderabadi biryani recipe I have made for years and has been a favorite among the readers of this blog. It is very easy and even a beginner can try without any troubles.

This is a kacchi hyderabadi biryani, meaning partially cooked rice is layered over the marinated raw meat.

To make the best out of this recipe, chicken needs a proper marination which mainly helps to tenderize it, resulting in juicy, flavorful, soft and tender chicken.

It can be marinated the previous night and refrigerated to make the best flavorful biryani.
You can serve hyderabadi biryani with any of these raitas I have shared earlier. Even lassi goes well in the meal.





Ingredients For Hyderabadi Biryani
  • One and Half cups of basmati rice
  • Half cup of chicken
  • Half teaspoon of biryani masala
  • One large onions
  • Four tablespoons coriander leaves
  • Four tablespoons mint leaves
  • Four tablespoons of ghee or OIl
  • Half teaspoons of saffron strands
